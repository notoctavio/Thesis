# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
This project welcomes respectful, constructive, and professional collaboration.

Examples of positive behavior:
- Giving clear, technical, and actionable feedback
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experience levels
- Focusing on improving the work, not criticizing people

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or insulting language
- Personal attacks and bad-faith interactions
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
